Forum Introduction

This forum is organized by the Institute for Advanced Study at Tsinghua University. Established in 1997, the Institute adheres to the principles of "innovation, interdisciplinarity, and integration," promoting a spirit of pioneering research and scientific originality. It focuses on research areas such as physics, cybersecurity, and astronomy that are of profound significance in exploring natural laws and have the potential to make critical contributions to future technological advancements. Committed to excellence, the Institute conducts fundamental scientific research. The Youth Scholars Interdisciplinary Forum aims to provide a platform for young scholars from diverse fields to engage in dialogue and collaboration, fostering discussions on frontier scientific questions and promoting academic breakthroughs and innovation.
